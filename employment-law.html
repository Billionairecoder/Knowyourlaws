Understanding Employment Law

Employment law covers workplace rights and obligations, ensuring fairness and safety for workers.

Employment law, also known as labor law, is a set of laws and regulations that govern the relationship between employees and employers. It covers many aspects of the workplace, including:
Rights and responsibilities
Employment law defines the rights and obligations of employees, employers, and union members.
Workplace safety
Employment law ensures that the workplace is safe and appropriate for work.
Hours and wages
Employment law governs the hours an employee can work and the wages they can receive.
Benefits
Employment law covers benefits such as family and medical leave, unemployment, and retirement benefits.
Discrimination
Employment law prohibits discrimination in the workplace.
